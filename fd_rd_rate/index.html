📌 MASTER INSTRUCTIONS & OFFICIAL RULES (PDF DATA)
1. FD/RD Interest Rules
  • Compounding: Quarterly compounded basis par interest calculate hota hai.
  • RD Logic: RD par bhi wahi rates apply hote hain jo FD par hain.
  • Minimum Tenure: ₹5 Lakh se kam FD ke liye minimum 15 din, usse upar 7 din.
  • NRE Rules: Minimum 1 saal tenure zaroori hai interest ke liye, warna 0% interest milega.
2. Age Criteria & Bonuses
  • Senior Citizens: 180 Days & above par extra 0.50% interest.
  • Super Seniors (80+): Sirf "Canara 444" aur "Canara 555" par extra 0.60% milta hai.
3. Tax & Penalty (Hisaab)
  • TDS Limit: ₹40k (Senior ₹50k) interest par TDS kategi. NRO par flat 31.2% tax hai.
  • PAN Effect: PAN nahi hone par 20% TDS kategi, PAN hone par 10%.
  • Form 15G/H: 15G (60 saal se kam) aur 15H (60 saal ya usse adhik) mein bharna zaroori hai tax bachane ke liye.
  • Penalty: Premature closure par 1.00% ki penalty rate se kam karke di jayegi.
  • Callable: Ye wo FD hoti hai jise aap waqt se pehle (prematurely) tudwa sakte hain.
  • Non-Callable: ₹1 Cr se upar ke deposits par premature allowed nahi hai par interest rates zyada hain.
